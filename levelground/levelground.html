WHERE GRIT MEETS OPPORTUNITY

At Level Ground (LG), urban youth cultivate grit and resilience through Mized Martial Arts. They learn to apply these values 'off the mat' in the pursuit of thier ambitions through our Student Trainer Certification and College Access programs.
